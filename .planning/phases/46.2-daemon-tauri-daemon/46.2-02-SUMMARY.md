## Decisions Made

- Used typed daemon client errors instead of parsing free-form strings in `uc-tauri`, because command-level UX mapping depends on stable `code/message` data from daemon responses.
- Kept frontend pairing classification in `src/api/p2p.ts`, not inside individual components, so initiator responses, passive command failures, and future command-error consumers share one normalization rule.
